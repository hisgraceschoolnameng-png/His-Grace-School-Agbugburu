About Us

Who we are

Our mission

Grounded in character, focused on learning

His Grace School Agbugburu exists to give children in Agbugburu Village and the wider Odeda Local Government area a solid academic foundation alongside strong moral character. We believe education should prepare a child for the classroom and for life.

What guides us

Our core values

1

Integrity

We teach pupils to be honest and accountable, in their schoolwork and in how they treat one another.

2

Diligence

Consistent effort is celebrated as much as natural talent — we help every child build good habits of work.

3

Respect

Pupils, staff, and families are treated with courtesy and dignity, building a genuinely caring school community.
